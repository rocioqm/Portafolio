Hi, I'm Rocio.


I'm a UX Designer by Laboratoria and a student of International Economics at National University of San Marcos. I'm a leader by nature with high sense of responsibility towards my work, others and the world around me. I like working in a team promoting effective communication. Also, I adapt easily to changes and always taking on each challenge with enthusiasm and passion.

I have been a National Representative in the regional final held in the city of Boston in 2017 in the contest of the Hult Prize Foundation, with the challenge: How to dignify the life of a refugee?. Achieving the TOP 10, among more than 50 teams from around the world, with the MAZI project.

I aspire to start my UX Designer career taking a challenging position in a company focused on the development of its employees through learning opportunities and professional growth. To be able to become a Growth Hacker and Product Manager very soon.
